How It Works

Photo intelligence in four steps — from shutter to action.

1

Capture

Choose your intent. Photo goes to iCloud Drive, never your Camera Roll.

2

Instant Recognition

Apple on-device AI kicks in immediately. OCR extracts text, barcodes are decoded, content is classified, foreign text is translated. No internet. No credits. Nothing leaves your phone.

3

Generative AI Insights

Auto-AI runs instantly for Food, Identify, Interpret, and Contact — the intents where it matters most. For everything else, tap Analyze when you want deeper insight. Each intent remembers your preference. One credit per analysis.

4

Act

Share to Reminders, Things, Contacts, Messages. Search across all folders. Browse on a map.

What Else Can It Do?

🧠

On-Device Intelligence — No Credits Needed

Every photo gets instant, private processing: full OCR with no character limit, barcode and QR code scanning, automatic classification and tagging, foreign text translation, and document edge detection. All on your phone. No internet required.

✂️

Document Auto-Crop

Detects document edges and offers one-tap perspective-corrected cropping. Great for receipts, business cards, and forms.

📐

Auto-Shrunk for Sharing

Photos compressed to ~300KB — small enough to email, text, or share anywhere. No more "file too large" errors.

🔎

Search Across Everything

Find any photo by title, text content, AI analysis, barcode data, or auto-tags. Works across all folders instantly.

🗓️

Filter by Time and Location

Open any folder and tap the filter icon to narrow by time period (Today, Yesterday, Last Week, Last Month) or by town. Combine both for precise browsing. Active filters show a dismissible banner so you always know what's filtered.

🌐

Works In Your Language

Translations and AI responses follow your system language. Override anytime in Settings.

🧹

Clean Your Camera Roll

Filter by album, screenshots, or photos not in any album. Select multiple and batch delete, move, or organize. Sort by similarity, document type, or location. Adjustable grid (1/2/3 columns). View photos on a map.

📁

Auto-File by Intent

Photos land in folders based on what you captured: Tasks, Read, Receipt, Food, Identify, Interpret, Slides, Reference, Contacts, or Evidence. Create custom folders in Files app—they appear automatically.

🤖

Smart Auto-AI

Auto-AI is configured per capture intent. Food, Identify, Interpret, and Contact default to ON — the intents where AI adds the most value. Toggle per intent on the SNAP screen; your choice is remembered.

⚡

Batch Mode

Rapid-fire capture without leaving camera. Snap 10 slides in a row, analyze them all later.

📤

Share to Any App

Reminders, Things 3, Notes, Messages, Mail—anywhere. Deep links jump back to the photo instantly.

📍

Location Tagged

Photos show where they were captured (e.g., "Boston 17Jan"). Know at a glance where you snapped that receipt.

📥

Import from Anywhere

Share Extension imports photos from any app. Clean up existing Camera Roll clutter too.

📦

Archive Without Moving

Mark items as archived to hide them from active view while keeping them in their folder. Toggle "Show Archived" to see them again.

🔀

Smart Sort Modes

Sort your Camera Roll by Documents First (finds document-like photos), Similar (groups visually alike photos), or by location coordinates.

💬

Follow-Up Questions

After AI analysis, ask Claude follow-up questions about the photo. Add context before analysis for better results.

📸

Screenshots Only Filter

Toggle Screenshots Only in the Roll tab to filter your Camera Roll to just screenshots. Combine with batch select for fast cleanup.

🗺️

Map View

Tap the map icon in the Roll tab to see your Camera Roll photos on a map. Photos with GPS data appear as pins — tap any pin to open the photo.
