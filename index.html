How It Works

Photo intelligence in four steps — from shutter to action.

1

Capture

Choose your intent. Photo goes to iCloud Drive, never your Camera Roll.

2

Instant Recognition

Apple on-device AI kicks in immediately. OCR extracts text, barcodes are decoded, content is classified, foreign text is translated. No internet. No credits. Nothing leaves your phone.

3

Generative AI Insights

Auto-AI runs instantly for Food, Identify, Interpret, and Contact — the intents where it matters most. For everything else, tap Analyze when you want deeper insight. Each intent remembers your preference. One credit per analysis.

4

Act

Share to Reminders, Things, Contacts, Messages. Search across all folders. Browse on a map.

Two Layers of Photo Intelligence

On-Device AI — Free. Instant. Private.

  • Text recognition (OCR) with no character limit
  • Barcode and QR code scanning
  • Content classification and auto-tagging
  • Foreign text translation
  • Document edge detection

Runs entirely on your iPhone — nothing leaves your device.

Generative AI — Deeper Understanding

  • Structures receipts into vendor, date, and total
  • Estimates calories and carbs from meal photos
  • Identifies unknown items with value estimates
  • Explains test results and jargon in plain language
  • Translates with cultural context, not just words
  • Compares similar photos to pick the best shot
  • Ask follow-up questions (first 2 free per analysis)

On-device AI does the heavy lifting. Generative AI adds the understanding. You always keep both.
