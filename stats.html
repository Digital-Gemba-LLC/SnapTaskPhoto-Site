Popular Locations

Your top 12 photography spots, with your #1 location (usually home) called out separately above the chart. Photos are grouped by geographic proximity (~11 km clusters) and reverse-geocoded to city or town names. Tap any bar to jump to photos from that location.

Timezone-Aware Hours

The "Photos by Hour" chart doesn't use your current timezone. It reverse-geocodes each geotagged photo's location to determine the actual local timezone where the photo was taken. A sunset photo in Paris shows up at Paris time, not your home timezone. Photos without GPS data use a stable GMT baseline.

Tap to explore. Tap any bar in the Quarter, Hour, or Location charts to jump to those photos in the Clean Up tab. A filter banner shows which selection is active — tap Clear to go back to the full view.

Heatmaps

Three bubble charts that cross-reference location, month, and year. Bigger bubbles mean more photos. Colors follow a Viridis-inspired gradient from dark purple (few) to yellow (many).

Locations by Month

Which locations you photograph in which months. Rows are your top locations, columns are Jan through Dec. Spot vacation patterns — the beach town that only lights up in July, the family visit every December. Tap any bubble to jump to those photos.

Locations by Year

How your location habits evolved over the last 10 years. See when you moved, when you started frequenting a new spot, or when you stopped visiting one. Tap any bubble to jump to those photos.

Photos by Year and Month

Your entire photo history as a single heatmap. Rows are years, columns are months. Instantly see your most active periods — and the gaps where life got busy or the phone stayed in your pocket.

How heatmaps are built

Unmuck groups your geotagged photos by geographic proximity (~11 km clusters), then reverse-geocodes each cluster to a city or town name. The top 13 locations are displayed. Bubble size uses cube-root scaling so small counts are still visible next to large ones. Colors follow a five-stop Viridis-inspired gradient from dark purple (fewest photos) to bright yellow (most).

Share Your Stats

Every section and chart can be shared individually as a high-resolution branded image.

How sharing works

  • Tap the share button on any section header or chart
  • A high-resolution image is generated at 3x scale
  • Each image includes the chart, a title, and a "Made with unmuck.app" watermark
  • If you've saved space, the watermark shows how much you've reclaimed
  • Share via the standard iOS share sheet to any app — Messages, Instagram, Twitter, etc.
Social-ready. Stats images are sized and branded for sharing. Show off your photography patterns, brag about reclaimed storage, or just marvel at how many screenshots you've accumulated.
