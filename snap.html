How SNAP Works

Three steps from shutter to organized.

1

Choose Your Intent

Tap what you're capturing: Receipt, Food, Contact, or any of the 10 intents. This tells the app how to process and file it.

2

Take the Photo

Capture directly to iCloud Drive. It never touches your Camera Roll. On-device AI kicks in instantly.

3

Instant Processing

Text is extracted, barcodes decoded, content classified. If Auto-AI is on, generative analysis runs too. Photo auto-files to the right folder.

Mode Toggles

Two toggles on the SNAP screen customize your capture workflow.

Auto-AI

Automatically runs generative AI analysis after capture. Configured per intent — Food, Identify, Interpret, and Contact default to ON. Each intent remembers your preference independently. Uses one credit per analysis.

Batch Mode

Keeps you in the camera after each capture for rapid-fire shooting. Great for snapping 10 slides in a row, or multiple receipts. Analyze them all later from the Folders tab.
